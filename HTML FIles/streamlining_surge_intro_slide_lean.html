Surge triage fails when risk is only visible late

Existing frameworks respond only after physiological decline is observable — missing the clinically actionable window.

The Problem

During surge conditions like COVID-19, patient volume exceeded ICU capacity — demanding effective allocation to treat all admitted at-risk patients.

increase in mortality odds from delayed ICU admission
Existing Frameworks Fail

NEWS2 and SOFA assess physiological vitals — lagging indicators of the underlying disease process.
